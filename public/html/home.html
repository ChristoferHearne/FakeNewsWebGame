The Fake News Web Game is a simple game where you will get 10 random articles, where its up to you to determine if its "Fake News" or not. You simply press the "Real" or "Fake" buttons to lock in your answer.

The articles are based on a dataset taken from politifact.com, where developers, through algorithms have determined the validity of a wide array of articles.
